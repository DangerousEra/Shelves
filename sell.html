Frequently Asked Questions

Is there really zero commission?
Yes! For our first 500 sellers, we're offering 0% commission for the first 6 months. After that, our commission rates start at just 5% — much lower than other platforms.
How quickly can I start selling?
Once approved, you can set up your store and list products in under 30 minutes. Our dashboard is designed to be intuitive and user-friendly.
What about payment processing?
We handle all payment processing securely through our partners. Payments are settled directly to your bank account within 24-48 hours of delivery.
Do I need to handle shipping myself?
You have options! Use our integrated shipping partners for discounted rates, or arrange your own shipping if you prefer. We provide tracking for all orders.
What kind of support do you provide?
All sellers get access to our seller support team via chat and email. Plus, we provide regular insights, marketing tips, and growth strategies.
