Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you respond to my message?
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent technical issues, we aim to respond within 4-6 hours.
Do you offer phone support?
Currently, we offer email and social media support for the fastest response times. Phone support is available for enterprise customers and urgent seller inquiries.
How do I apply to sell on Shelves?
You can apply through our Sell on Shelves page or email us at sell@theshelves.in with your business details. We'll review your application within 48 hours.
Where is your office located?
Our headquarters is in Bangalore, India. However, we're a remote-first company with team members across India. All meetings are currently virtual.
Do you offer internships?
Yes! We offer internships in design, development, marketing, and business development. Send your resume and portfolio to careers@theshelves.in.
